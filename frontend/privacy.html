Privacy

What we do not store

  • Submitted URLs.
  • Email subjects, senders, or bodies.
  • Your IP address in plaintext.

What we do store (briefly)

  • A hashed IP for up to 24 hours, only to enforce rate limits.
  • Aggregate counts (number of checks per day). Never linked to you.

Third parties

URL checks forward the URL to Google Safe Browsing, VirusTotal and PhishTank for reputation lookups. Email checks send the content to Groq for AI analysis. Review their policies if this matters to you.
